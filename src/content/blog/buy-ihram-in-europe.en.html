Where to buy Ihram in Europe

European pilgrims have three main options:

  • Local Islamic shops, usually present in larger cities with a Muslim population. Pricing varies, and stock is inconsistent close to Hajj season.
  • International online vendors, often shipping from the Middle East or South Asia. Pricing can be lower, but shipping takes 2 to 4 weeks and customs duties may apply for orders over EU thresholds.
  • European online vendors, the smaller category, but the most reliable for pilgrims who want their set to arrive on time. Pure Ihram falls into this category.
